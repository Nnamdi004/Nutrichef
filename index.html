Spicy Thai Green Curry with Tofu

This recipe delivers authentic Thai flavor with a healthy, plant-based twist. Ready in under an hour, it's a perfect weeknight meal.

Prep Time20 min
Cook Time20 min
Servings4
Rating4.9 / 5

Ingredients

1 tbsp coconut oil
1 block firm tofu, cubed
4 oz green curry paste
1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers)
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves

Instructions

1

Heat coconut oil in a large pot or wok. Add the tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove and set aside.

2

Add the green curry paste to the pot and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.

3

Add mixed vegetables and simmer for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp. Return the tofu to the pot and heat through.

4

Stir in fresh basil leaves, season with salt and lime juice to taste, and serve immediately over rice or noodles.
